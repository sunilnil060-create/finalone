Small Portraits of a Vast Cultural World

Ethnographic vignettes are short, closely observed accounts of everyday moments — a lamp being lit, a story told over butter tea, a skill passed from elder to child. Each vignette weaves together text, images, and sound into a living portrait of highland Bhutanese life.
